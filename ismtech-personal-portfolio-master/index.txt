About me

I'm Civil Engineering Student at Auchi Poly. and A Self-Taught web developer, I was able to learn HTML, CSS, javascript, PHP, SQL including Python programming language on my own while I was still in Higher school in  Nigeria, working in web development, . I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive designs.

My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. I created website like hospital management system, Resturant management system include schooh website and many more....
